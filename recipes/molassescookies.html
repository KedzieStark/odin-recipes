Molasses Ginger Cookies

Description

Chewy molasses cookies, with a spicy kick of ginger!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 c (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 c molasses
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 3 c AP flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Beat butter and sugar together til light and fluffy.
  2. Scrape down sides of bowl and add the ginger, egg, and molasses. Continue to mix until thoroughly blended.
  3. Sift in the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Mix until just barely combined. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hr.
  4. When ready to bake, preheat oven to 350Â°f. Roll dough into 3/4 in balls. Roll dough balls in sugar and place on a parchment paper lined baking sheet about 2 in apart.
  5. Bake 7-8 min, rotating halfway through.

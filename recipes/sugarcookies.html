Sugar Cookies

Description

Classic cutout sugar cookies. Traditionally made for Christmas, but they're great any time of year!

Ingredients

  • 3 c AP flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 c (two sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tbs milk
  • 1 tsp molasses
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 c sugar

Steps

  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add milk, egg, molasses, and vanilla to the butter-sugar mixture. Stir to thoroughly combine.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients.
  5. Divide dough into two portions. Wrap with plastic wrap, chill for ~2 hr in fridge.
  6. Roll dough out to approx 1/4 in thick. Cut out desired shapes.
  7. Transfer to a parchment-paper lined baking sheet and bake for 7-9 min at 375Â°f, or until just slightly browned around the edges.
